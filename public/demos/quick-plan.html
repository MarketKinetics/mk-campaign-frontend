    Methodology & notes how figures are modeled, and notes on this plan
    How figures are modeled

    Conversion projected on the funnel-narrowed conversion-stage audience (7,088 qualified), not the full audience — the accurate denominator.

    [A-PRIORI] Combined de-duplicated reach via random-duplication (assumes channels reach people independently — the standard media-planning default when cross-media panel data is absent; a slight over-estimate, so a conservative upper bound). Real duplication requires cross-media panel data.

    [A-PRIORI] Always-on (flight) channels counted as expected impressions at 2.5/person/week (conservative; all-digital avg ~3.4, Facebook median ~2.51).

    Funnel is a TIME-PHASED COHORT model, not per-person targeting. Per-person stage targeting requires a behavioral CDP (downstream of this plan).

    Projected conversions are a conservative FLOOR from benchmarked channels only; true brand/reach-only channels (radio/TV/OOH/earned) add unquantified upside.

    Rates are UNIVERSAL [A-PRIORI] benchmarks; real conversion varies widely by domain, offer, creative, audience. Override with your own rates.
